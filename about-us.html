We Are Max Estates

Building spaces that allow you to live more at work and do more at home

Founded in 2016, Max Estates Limited has a rich history of designing and developing world-class projects, and our commitment to your well-being has been a driving force behind each of our properties. We are forever on a quest to create innovative, sustainable, and socially responsible projects that redefine living spaces and workspaces for you.

Our Purpose

Enhance quality of life through spaces we create

Our Mission

Established in 2016, the purpose of the organisation is to ‘enhance quality of life through spaces we create’. We endeavour to achieve it through our mission of:

  • Augmenting the quality of life through exceptional design, sustainability, and experiences
  • Being the most preferred choice for all stakeholders including customers, communities, shareholders, and employees
  • Building a great place to work that attracts, nurtures and retains exceptional people
  • Leading the market in harnessing technology to deliver world class spaces
  • Maintaining cutting edge standards of governance

Journey Of Max Estates Limited

2016-2018

Explore

The inception of Max Estates 222 Rajpur, a premier residential community, consisting of 22 Villas, located at adjacent to the Malsi forest, on the most exclusive address in Dehradun was launched as the first boutique development

2018-2021

Establish

Formalized real estate strategy to one region, multiple asset class focus on Delhi NCR as a region and CRE as the asset class. Successfully launched, completed, and 100% leased out our first two Grade A+ commercial developments – Max Towers and Max House

2022 Onwards

Expand

Announced acquisition of multiple land parcels across Delhi NCR, diversified across regions and asset classes. The company expands its footprint and establishes itself as one of the most prominent developers in North India
