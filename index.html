More About Us

Max Estates Limited, real estate development arm of the Max Group, is a leading real estate developer in India, with a focus on creating sustainable and world-class developments. With over four decades of expertise in handling some of India's largest businesses across multiple verticals, we are committed to creating developments that are not just functional and aesthetically pleasing, but environmentally sustainable too.

Skipping Ahead

Philosophy

MEL's philosophy is rooted in the belief that the spaces we inhabit have a profound impact on our well-being. We recognise that wellness is about more than just physical health, and that our environment plays a crucial role in our overall sense of well-being.

Empathetic Hospitality

An intelligent approach to hospitality and service is by creating one that values empathy. We aim at doing our best by understanding the emotional and social needs of our customers, and creating spaces where they feel cared for.

Environmental Harmony

We build spaces that offer care and well-being for every form of life on the planet and not just human beings. Our properties are designed to utilize natural light and air to the fullest, along with sustainable practices like an expansive green cover,  rainwater harvesting and groundwater recharging.

Generosity

We believe in the generosity of time, by planning from all angles before execution. We’re generous in our attention to detail, since it is the small things that contribute the most to the larger picture. We believe in the generosity of space by offering more green cover per person.

Intentional Design

Our design interventions are carefully crafted to promote a healthier lifestyle. We offer comfort with the right ergonomics, acoustics and thermal considerations. We ensure beauty in design by placing inspiring art at regular intervals. We encourage physical movement by offering wide, open, inviting spaces.

Inclusivity

As a company that believes in caring for our consumers, we ensure that nobody feels left out. Our spaces are designed for the well-being and free movement of all residents, irrespective of age, gender, ability and circumstances. 

Peace Of Mind

Through safety and security powered by the best in class safety measures, we minimize worries for our customers. We intentionally select locations to offer supreme connectivity and convenience. We ensure the highest standards of cleanliness and sanitation through building management, even after handover. 

Belonging

Each of our properties have specially designed amenities for engagement among residents and to promote social well-being. The amenities help our customers choose a happier, more active lifestyle. Our amenities almost always include a gym, separate areas for sporting and leisurely activities and multiple entertainment zones.

Sustainability

The use of sustainable materials, recycling everything that can be reused and careful resource conservation at every step of the way - these are habits that differentiate us from most other real estate companies.

Food and Nurtition

We care for the health and well-being of our customers by offering easy access to organic vegetables and biotic food sales. We also offer curated F&B options with extreme focus on quality and nutrition.
